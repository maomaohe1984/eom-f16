Assignment #7

Spatial Interactive Experience:
The Software Mirror

  • Course Home

Create a digital mirror which reflects a user’s form while creating an interactive and aesthetically interesting experience. Consider integrating sound, kinetic and dynamic forms using the image processing and analysis techniques we’ve explored. Consider the narrative of the experience you are trying to create for your users. How does the experience unfold over time?

Install your digital mirror in a public location. Document, using stills and video, users interacting with your creation.

Adapted from Alison Kotin's & Nicole Tariverdian's Software Mirror assignment.

Deliverables

Sync all code deliverables to your github student repository, and all videos to YouTube by 6:30pm on the due date. Please link to all your YouTube videos from your README.md file.

  1. Your Digital Mirror Sketch or Patch source code.
  2. Video documentation of users interacting with your Software Mirror.
